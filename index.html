Travel Tracking System

This Travel Tracking System is a system that is used by staff members and heads of units. Its purpose is to enable a staff member to create a travel plan when going for a mission and submit it to a supervisor or head of unit who then either approves or rejects. The staff member will be able to review the travel plan from the supervisor, update it, or even submit an online mission report.
